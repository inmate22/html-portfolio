KGF

Indian film from the Kannad Industry which made way for dark action films. The movie starring Rocking star Yash as the protagansit Rocky was an instant hit in theaters all across the country. The protagansit fillled with the ambition of becoming the most powerful person in the entire world is faced with obstacles of the underworld that controls the Kolar Gold Fields which is the bigest gold mines. Rocky sets on a path to eliminate the entire top heads and becomes the sole ruler of KGF. Witnessing its mindblowing and beat stopping climax still gives me chills. Wish I could experience it all again.
